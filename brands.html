OUR PRODUCTS

We offer a variety of poultry products including eggs, Day-Old chicks, broiler meat in different packs, family packs, chicken fillets, and chicken wings. Our products are available in various formats to suit your needs

Table Eggs

We produce high-quality poultry eggs with exceptional nutritional value, sourced from a clean and serene environment. Our advanced feed mill system ensures efficient nutrient transfer from feed to egg, guaranteeing top-notch quality.

Dayntee Chicks

We offer premium Day-Old chicks raised in a clean and controlled environment. Our advanced care and nutrition protocols ensure healthy, robust chicks with optimal growth potential, thanks to the precise feed formulations we use

Poultry Birds

We provide high-quality poultry birds raised in a pristine and controlled environment. Our meticulous management and advanced feeding practices ensure robust, healthy birds with exceptional growth and performance.

Family Pack

Our family packs feature a selection of premium poultry products, including broiler meat, chicken fillets, and wings. Conveniently packaged to cater to your family's needs, each pack offers quality and value for everyday meals.

Chicken Fillet

OOur chicken fillets are tender, juicy, and expertly processed to ensure premium quality. Perfect for a variety of dishes, they provide a delicious and nutritious option for your meals.

Chicken Wings

Our chicken wings are flavorful and succulent, expertly prepared to ensure top quality. Ideal for grilling, baking, or frying, they offer a delicious and versatile choice for your culinary needs.

DAYNTEE FARM INFORMATION

Dayntee Farms is a state-of-the-art integrated poultry operation, specializing in the production of commercial table eggs, egg powder, and broilers.

Our vision is to become Nigeria's leading brand in layer poultry and egg production.

Our mission is to lead the Nigerian market in table egg production by adhering to world-class standards of bio-security and quality, utilizing locally sourced raw materials to ensure sustainable food security and contribute to national economic development.
