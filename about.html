Leading Agri-Tech Innovators in North Central Nigeria: Pioneering High-Quality Poultry Production for Global Food Security

One of the Fastest agri-tech Organization in North central Nigeria that engages in producing high quality and healthy poultry products to Nigerians, focused towards maximum contribution to global food security.

We engaged services of qualified professionals in the field of veterinary medicine, animal science, engineering, accounting, marketing and business administration, managing the farm operations which comprise of breeder farm, hatchery, feed milling, broiler fattening, live bird processing plant and Day-Old chicks production.

Our Story

At Dayntee Farms, we believe in the power of growth and transformation. Established in 2011, we have rapidly emerged as a pioneer in the poultry industry. Our journey began with commercial egg production, and through relentless innovation, we evolved into a thriving poultry breeding and hatching operation.

By 2017, our passion for excellence led us to expand into broiler meat processing, proudly introducing our flagship product, Ivory Chicken. This premium line exemplifies our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Join us as we continue to nurture dreams, embrace progress, and cultivate a future filled with promise and prosperity. At Dayntee Farms, we're more than just a farm; we're a movement towards a better tomorrow.
